# Gaz Express Context

Status: Draft
Project: Gaz Express Conversion OS

## Current Project Context

Gaz Express is a placeholder name for an LPG supplier campaign.

The immediate need is to introduce the company to new prospects in the restaurant and hospitality sector.

A field representative will usually have already visited, cold-called, or collected the owner's details.

The digital campaign must then convert the prospect into a conversation and ultimately into a client.

## Target Market

Primary market:

- restaurants
- takeaways
- hotels
- guesthouses
- coffee shops
- event venues
- hospitality operators

## Current Asset Ideas

- WhatsApp introduction graphic
- mobile-first landing page
- company profile PDF
- follow-up WhatsApp sequence

## Current Strategic Decision

The page must build credibility and confidence.

The conversion path must loop back into WhatsApp.

A traditional form-first lead capture flow is not the right default for this market.

## Core Campaign Question

Can I trust Gaz Express to keep my kitchen running?

## Current Conversion Stack

1. Representative visit or cold call.
2. First WhatsApp introduction.
3. WhatsApp graphic introduces value proposition.
4. Prospect clicks mobile landing page.
5. Landing page builds confidence.
6. Prospect clicks Get Pricing.
7. WhatsApp opens with pre-filled message.
8. Sales rep continues the conversation.
9. Quote is generated.
10. Follow-up engine keeps prospect warm.

## Open Questions

- What real company proof is available?
- Which service areas should be shown?
- Are there existing hospitality customers that can be named?
- Are there testimonials or Google reviews?
- Does Gaz Express offer emergency supply?
- What pricing information can be discussed publicly?
- Who receives the WhatsApp leads?
- Should leads route to one central number or the original rep?
